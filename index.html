    2022 Tournament May 7-8 (no meeting May 8)

    Steve

    2022-04-09 16:25

    The 2022 Calgary Go Tournament will be held the weekend of May 7-8. There will be no regular club meeting May 8, as we'll be busy with the tournament.

    Location: Calgary Co-op Brentwood
    4122 Brentwood Rd NW
    View Map
    Date and Time: Saturday May 7 and Sunday May 8
    9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
    Registration: Email Vincent at studentofsmith@gmail.com with your name and rank (if known)
    Entry fee: NONE!
    Co-op does not charge us for use of the community room, so we have negligible costs to run the tournament.
    So come have some fun and play some games!
    Prizes: Fame and glory!

    The tournament format will be 6 round Swiss McMahon, with divisions to be finalized the day of the tournament. We will use AGA rules, with 45 minutes main time per side, 25 stones in 10 minutes Canadian byo-yomi.

    The time table shall be approximately:

    Saturday, May 7
    9:00 AM Doors open
    10:00 AM First Round
    12:00 PM Lunch Break
    1:00 PM Second Round
    3:00 PM Third Round
    6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Optional socialization, games for fun
    Sunday, May 8
    9:00 AM Doors open
    10:00 AM Fourth Round
    12:00 PM Lunch Break
    1:00 PM Fifth Round
    3:00 PM Sixth Round
    6:00 PM Awards Ceremony
    6:30 PM - 8:00 PM Optional socialization, games for fun

    Please contact Vincent if you have any further questions. See you there!

    Calgary Go Club meetings resume August 29, 2021

    Steve

    2021-08-02 15:04

    Well, it's been a long time but we are now cleared to resume regular Go Club meetings!

    Meetings shall resume at the Calgary Central Library, 12-5pm Sundays. The first day back is August 29th, 2021.

    All attendees are encouraged to get their COVID vaccines, to protect themselves and everyone else. The pandemic hasn't gone away, but the more of us that are vaccinated, the safer we'll all be.

    And of course, not everyone will be comfortable having social gatherings at this point in time. Only consider attending if you feel comfortable with it!

    Omatsuri 2019 - August 10

    Steve

    2019-07-29 07:51

    It's already time for the 9th annual "Omatsuri" Japanese Festival!

    As has become tradition, the Calgary Go Club will be running a table, teaching beginners to play. Vincent and Steve will be running the table but any players are welcome to join, learn, teach, and chat.

    Details:

    • Who
      • Anyone who wants to play or learn Go!
    • When
      • Saturday, August 10, 2019, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
    • Where
      • Max Bell Centre, 1001 Barlow Trail SE (map)

    Parking is limited, so if you can arrive by transit, that may make your travel easier.
