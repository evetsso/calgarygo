    Omatsuri 2024 - August 9 + 10

    Steve

    2024-06-10 20:49

    The Calgary Japanese Festival Omatsuri 2024 is coming! This year's event will be spread across two days at Max Bell Centre.

    The evening of Friday August 9 will have some modern events, while all day Saturday August 10 will include more traditional cultural events. The Calgary Go Club will be there the whole time for anyone that wants to learn or play Go!

    • Who
      • Anyone who wants to play or learn Go!
    • When
      • Friday, August 9, 2024, 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM
      • Saturday, August 10, 2024, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
    • Where
      • Max Bell Centre, 1001 Barlow Trail SE (map)

    As always, parking is limited and attendees should prefer to use public transit where possible.
