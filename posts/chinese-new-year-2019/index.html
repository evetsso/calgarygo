    Chinese New Year 2019, Feb 3+10 meetings moved to 2+9

    Steve

    2019-01-13 22:31

    Our hosts, the Calgary Jiang Zhe Shanghai Association, will be using our space for Chinese New Year celebrations during our regular meeting times on February 3 and 10. Go club meetings are cancelled for those dates.

    However, the space is available on both Saturdays @ 6:30. A bunch of us will be getting together on the 2nd and 9th in lieu of regular meetups on the 3rd and 10th.

    Happy year of the pig!
