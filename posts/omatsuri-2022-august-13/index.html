    Omatsuri 2022 - August 13

    Steve

    2022-08-05 10:58

    After a pandemic hiatus, the Calgary Japanese Festival "Omatsuri" is back!

    The Calgary Go Club will once again run a table, teaching beginners to play. Vincent and Steve will be running the table but any players are welcome to join, learn, teach, and chat.

    Details:

    • Who
      • Anyone who wants to play or learn Go!
    • When
      • Saturday, August 13, 2022, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
    • Where
      • Max Bell Centre, 1001 Barlow Trail SE (map)

    Limited parking is available, so transit is preferred if possible. Come play Go, or enjoy all of the other activities, performances, and foods that the festival has to offer!
